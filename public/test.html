macOS Auto-Scroll Test Page

Instructions

This page is designed to test the macOS Auto-Scroll extension. Try the following:

  1. Press the middle mouse button anywhere on this page to activate auto-scroll
  2. Move your mouse relative to the circle to control scroll direction and speed
  3. Click any mouse button or press Escape to deactivate auto-scroll
  4. Try auto-scrolling in the nested scrollable areas below
